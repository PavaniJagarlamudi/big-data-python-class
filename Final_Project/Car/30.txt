In 2014, BMW developed a prototype of street lights equipped with power sockets to charge electric cars, called Light and Charge.[66] Two of these charging facilities were installed at BMW's headquarters in Munich.[67]
In 2015, BMW in cooperation with SCHERM Group has started deploying electric trucks on European roads, making it the first company to ever do so. The truck itself is manufactured by the Terberg Group, one of the world's largest independent specialist vehicle suppliers.[68][69][70]
