Buckler was Managing Director of Bucklers of Reading Ltd and Buckler Cars Ltd. He was born on 26 September 1910 in Hornsey, London. Buckler was educated at Tottenham Grammar School and S Thomas College. From 1927 to 1929 he trained as an engineer with Frank Buckler Ltd. Followed by working for the legal firm Crossman Block and Co to 1933. In 1934 he joined Watford Dairies as a Transport and Sales Manager. He became a Director of the engineering firm Johnson Roberts Ltd in 1939 followed by W C Ford Ltd, coach builders until 1956, and Rivers Motors (Reading) Ltd from 1945.
